Pumpkin Spice Coffee Cake Recipe

Home

Description

A soft, cozy coffee cake filled with warm punmpkin spice flavor and a sweet cinnamon-sugar ribbon throughout. Perfect for fall mornings or holiday gatherings. Best enjoyed with a hot cup of coffee or hot chocolate!

Ingredients

    For the Cake

  • Cooking Spray
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon sugar (for coating the pan)
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 ½ tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup sour cream

    • For the Filling

  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp ground cinnamon

Steps To Perfection

    General Note

  • Prep Time: ~15 mins
  • Cook Time: ~50 mins
  • Total Time: ~1 hr 5 mins
  1. Prepare the Pan

    2. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Spray a 10-inch Bundt or tube pan with cooking spray and coat with cinnamon sugar.

  2. Mix Wet Ingredients

    3. In a large bowl, beat the sugar and butter together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, then mix in the pumpkin pie spice and vanilla.

  3. Add Dry Ingredients & Sour Cream

    4. In a seperate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and baking soda. Add the dry mixture to the wet in small portions, alternating with sour cream. Begin and end with the flour mixture.

  4. Add the Cinnamon Filling

    5. Combine the brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Pour ⅓ of the cake batter into the prepared pan, spring half of the cinnamon mixture on top, then repeat with another layer. Finish with the remaining batter.

  5. Bake

    6. Bake for 8 minutes then lower the oven temperature to 350°F (175°C) and continue baking for about 40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

  6. Cool & Serve

    7. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove from the pan to cool fully before slicing. Dust with powder sugar or icing if desired.
