Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

Experience the fomrot of a classic Italian favorite with this hearty, made-from-scratch lasagna. Layered with rich & savory meat sauce, tender lasagna noodles and a creamy blend of cheeses. This dish deliveres the perfect balance of flavor in each bite.

Ingredients

    Meat Sauce

  • ½ lb ground pork
  • ½ lb lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (divided)
  • 1 ½ tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp white sugar

    • Cheese Filling

  • 1 lb small-curd cottage cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 Tbsp fresh parsley (remaining from above)
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp salt

    • Pasta & Toppings

  • 1 (16 oz) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 (16 oz) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps To Perfection

    General Note

  • Prep Time: ~1 hr 25 mins
  • Cook Time: ~1 hr 15 mins
  • Total Time: ~2 hrs 50 mins
  1. Brown the Meat

    2. Cook the ground pork and ground beef together in a large skillet over medium-high heat until fully brown and crumbly.

  2. Add Aromatics and Sauce Ingredients

    3. Stir in the chopped onion and cook until softened. Mix cruushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, half of the parsley garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat and let the sauce gently simmer for about 30 minutes,stirring occasionaly

  3. Cook the Noodles

    4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles until they are tender, but still firm to the bite. Drain and set aside.

  4. Prepare the Cheese Filling

    5. In a mixing bowl, combine cottage cheese, parmesan, eggs and the remaining parsely, salt, and peper. Stir until blended well.

  5. Preheat the Oven

    6. Heat the oven to 375°F (190°C) while you prepare to assemble

  6. Assemble the Lasagna

    7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Add two layers of noodles, followed by half of the cheese mixture, half of the remaining sauce, and half of the mozzarella. Repeat the layering once more.

  7. Bake

    8. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 30-40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the top is bubbling and golden.

  8. Rest and Serve

    9. Let the lasagna sit for 10 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set and makes serving easier.
